PACMAN

Instructions

The game consists of two options:


1. Play Game

You can play the game yourself.
Press Left, Right, Up, Down Key to Move Pacman.

2. RL Simulation

Watch Pacman learn to play itself.
Approximate Q Learning is used by Pacman to learn the game. It consists of 50 iterations where the first 40 are training phases and the last 10 is testing phase.
